## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the application URL

## Employee Guide

### Applying for Leave

#### Step 1: Navigate to Apply Leave
1. Click "Apply for Leave" from dashboard or navigation menu
2. Or use the quick action button in the top right

#### Step 2: Select Leave Type

Choose from available leave types:
- **Earned Leave (EL):** Annual leave, accrues 2 days/month
- **Casual Leave (CL):** Short-term personal leave, 14 days/year
- **Medical Leave (ML):** For illness, 14 days/year
- **Maternity Leave:** 16 weeks for eligible employees
- **Paternity Leave:** 10 working days
- **Study Leave:** For approved courses
- **Special Disability Leave:** For work-related injuries
- **Quarantine Leave:** For mandatory quarantine
- And more...

#### Step 3: Select Dates
1. Click on the date range picker
2. Select start date and end date
3. System automatically calculates working days (excluding weekends and holidays)
4. View your projected balance after this leave

#### Step 4: Provide Reason
- Enter a clear, concise reason for your leave
- Minimum 10 characters required
- Be specific for medical/special leave types

#### Step 5: Upload Documents (if required)

**Medical Leave > 3 days:**
- Medical certificate required
- Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG
- Maximum size: 5MB

**Maternity Leave:**
- Medical certificate confirming pregnancy
- Expected delivery date certificate

**Study Leave:**
- Admission letter / registration proof
- Loan/scholarship agreement (if applicable)
- Course fee receipt (optional)
- Course details (name, institution, duration)

#### Step 6: Review and Submit
1. Check the summary panel on the right
2. Verify all details are correct
3. Click "Submit Request"
4. You'll receive a confirmation notification

### Tracking Your Leave Requests

#### View Leave History
1. Go to "My Leaves" from the navigation
2. See all your leave requests with statuses:
   - **DRAFT:** Not yet submitted
   - **SUBMITTED:** Awaiting first approval
   - **PENDING:** In approval process
   - **APPROVED:** Approved and confirmed
   - **REJECTED:** Not approved
   - **RETURNED:** Needs modification
   - **CANCELLED:** Cancelled by you or admin

#### Check Approval Status
1. Click on any leave request
2. See approval timeline with:
   - Current approval stage
   - Who has approved
   - Who needs to approve next
   - Comments from approvers

### Checking Leave Balance

#### View Current Balances
1. Go to "Balance" from navigation
2. See breakdown for each leave type:
   - **Opening:** Balance at start of year
   - **Accrued:** Earned during the year
   - **Used:** Taken so far
   - **Closing:** Current available balance

#### Understanding Conversions
- If Earned Leave exceeds 60 days, excess converts to Special Leave
- Special Leave can be used for medical purposes or rest outside Bangladesh
- Conversions are shown in your balance view
- Contact HR if you have questions about conversions

### Modifying Leave Requests

#### Before Approval Starts
1. Open the leave request
2. Click "Edit" or "Cancel"
3. Make changes and resubmit

#### After Submission (Returned for Modification)
1. You'll receive notification when returned
2. Open the request
3. Make requested changes
4. Upload any missing documents
5. Click "Resubmit"

### Cancelling Leave

#### Before Leave Starts
1. Open approved leave request
2. Click "Cancel Leave"
3. Provide cancellation reason
4. Submit cancellation request
5. Wait for approval

#### After Leave Starts (Partial Cancellation)
1. Open active leave request
2. Click "Partial Cancel"
3. Enter new end date (earlier than original)
4. Provide reason
5. Submit request

### Extending Leave

If you need to extend approved leave:
1. Open the original leave request
2. Click "Request Extension"
3. Enter new end date
4. Provide medical/valid reason
5. Upload supporting documents
6. Submit extension request

**Note:** Extension creates a linked request that goes through approval process separately.

### Shortening Leave

If you want to return early:
1. Open approved leave request
2. Click "Shorten Leave"
3. Enter earlier end date
4. Provide reason
5. Submit request

### Viewing Company Holidays

1. Go to "Holidays" from navigation
2. Switch between:
   - **Calendar View:** Visual month grid with holidays highlighted
   - **List View:** Traditional list of all holidays
3. Filter by:
   - Holiday category (Public, Restricted, Festival)
   - Month range
   - Show/hide weekends
4. Export holiday calendar as PDF for printing

### Uploading Fitness Certificate

**Required for Medical Leave > 7 days:**

1. After returning from medical leave > 7 days
2. You'll see "Upload Fitness Certificate" prompt
3. Upload certificate from registered medical practitioner
4. HR will review and approve
5. You cannot take new medical leave until approved

## Department Head Guide

As a Department Head, you can manage your team's leave requests and view team availability.

### Viewing Team Leaves

1. Go to "Team Overview" from dashboard
2. See all team members' leave requests
3. Filter by status, date, or employee
4. View team calendar to see who's on leave when

### Approving Leave Requests

#### Review Process
1. Go to "Approvals" or click notification
2. Review request details:
   - Employee name and department
   - Leave type and dates
   - Reason provided
   - Current balance
   - Team availability during those dates
3. Check for conflicts or staffing issues

#### Approval Actions

**To Approve:**
1. Click "Approve"
2. Add comment (optional)
3. Confirm approval
4. Employee and next approver notified

**To Reject:**
1. Click "Reject"
2. Provide clear reason for rejection
3. Confirm rejection
4. Employee notified immediately

**To Return for Modification:**
1. Click "Return for Modification"
2. Specify what needs to be changed
3. Submit feedback
4. Employee can modify and resubmit

**To Forward:**
1. Click "Forward"
2. Select recipient (usually HR Head or CEO)
3. Add comment explaining why
4. Submit forward

### Team Capacity Planning

1. Use team calendar view
2. See all approved and pending leaves
3. Identify potential understaffing periods
4. Plan accordingly before approving new requests
5. Use heatmap view to see high-demand periods

### Bulk Actions

For efficiency:
1. Select multiple leave requests (checkboxes)
2. Click "Bulk Approve" or "Bulk Return"
3. Add single comment for all
4. Confirm action

## HR Admin Guide

As HR Admin, you're the first line of review for all leave requests.

### Processing Leave Requests

#### Your Responsibilities
1. Verify leave balance availability
2. Check policy compliance
3. Validate required documents
4. Ensure all fields are complete
5. Forward to Department Head or approve directly (for standard cases)

#### Document Verification

**Medical Certificates:**
- Check certificate is from registered practitioner
- Verify dates match leave request
- Ensure certificate clearly states fitness for duty
- Reject if certificate is unclear or invalid

**Maternity Certificates:**
- Confirm pregnancy documentation
- Check expected delivery date
- Verify 16-week entitlement calculation
- Check for any prior maternity leaves

**Study Leave Documents:**
- Admission letter verification
- Loan agreement review (if applicable)
- Course duration matches leave request
- Retirement date check (must serve 2x study period)

#### Soft Override

For minor policy violations you can override:
1. Click "Approve with Override"
2. Document reason for override
3. Add justification comment
4. Submit (logs in audit trail)

**Use cases for soft override:**
- 1-2 day balance shortage with good reason
- Minor notice period violations
- Emergency situations

### Managing Fitness Certificates

1. Go to "Fitness Certificates" section
2. Review uploaded certificates
3. Verify authenticity
4. Approve or request resubmission
5. Track pending fitness certificate uploads

### Handling Info Requests

1. Receive requests from employees or approvers
2. Respond with policy guidance
3. Provide balance clarifications
4. Assist with document uploads

### Managing Conversions

1. Review Earned Leave balances monthly
2. Identify employees with EL > 60 days
3. System auto-suggests conversions
4. Process conversion to Special Leave
5. Notify employee

### Audit & Compliance

1. Use audit log to track all actions
2. Review override usage
3. Monitor policy violations
4. Generate compliance reports
5. Flag suspicious patterns

## HR Head Guide

As HR Head, you handle escalations and strategic oversight.

### Strategic Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- Company-wide leave trends
- Department-wise utilization
- Policy exception requests
- Pending escalations
- Budget impact analysis

### Approving Special Cases

You approve:
1. **Extended Leave:** > 30 days
2. **Policy Exceptions:** Soft overrides escalated from HR Admin
3. **Study Leave:** All study leave requests
4. **Special Disability:** Compensation and duration
5. **CEO Leave:** Forwarded for final approval

### Study Leave Management

#### Evaluation Criteria
1. Course relevance to CDBL operations
2. Employee service record
3. Retirement date (must serve 2x study period)
4. Budget availability
5. Replacement plan

#### Approval Process
1. Review admission documents
2. Check loan/scholarship agreement
3. Verify service bond calculations
4. Approve or request CEO approval (> 12 months)

### Handling Appeals

If employee appeals rejection:
1. Review original request and rejection reason
2. Evaluate new information provided
3. Consult with department head
4. Make final decision
5. Document decision thoroughly

### Policy Management

1. Access policy configuration
2. Propose policy changes
3. Update leave type rules
4. Adjust accrual rates (if authorized)
5. Monitor policy effectiveness

### Reports & Analytics

#### Generate Reports
1. Go to "Reports" section
2. Select date range
3. Choose filters (department, leave type, etc.)
4. Generate report
5. Export as PDF or Excel

#### Key Metrics to Monitor
- Leave utilization rate by department
- Average approval time
- Rejection rates and reasons
- Balance carryover trends
- Cost implications

## Best Practices

### For Employees
- Apply for leave at least 7 days in advance
- Keep documents ready before applying
- Check balance before requesting
- Provide clear, honest reasons
- Upload valid medical certificates
- Plan around company holidays

### For Approvers
- Review requests within 2 business days
- Provide constructive feedback when rejecting
- Check team availability before approving
- Document override reasons thoroughly
- Communicate with employees proactively

### For HR Staff
- Maintain up-to-date policy documentation
- Respond to queries promptly
- Verify documents carefully
- Keep audit trail complete
- Monitor for policy violations
